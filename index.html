Our Story

Traditional Recipes, Modern Convenience

Welcome to Thara's Kitchen, where we bring the authentic flavors of Indian home cooking to your kitchen. Our journey began in a small family kitchen, where Thara would prepare traditional spice blends and pickles using recipes passed down through generations.

What started as a passion for preserving culinary heritage has now become a mission to share these authentic flavors with food lovers everywhere. Each product is carefully crafted with the same love and attention to detail that goes into homemade preparations.

We source only the finest ingredients and follow traditional methods to ensure every bite takes you back to the comforting flavors of home-cooked meals.

Puliyogare Powder

₹199

Our signature tamarind rice spice blend is a carefully crafted mixture of premium spices, dried red chilies, coriander seeds, fenugreek, mustard, and other secret ingredients that bring the perfect balance of tangy, spicy and sweet flavors to your rice dishes.

Benefits

  • Rich in antioxidants from tamarind and spices
  • Contains digestive aids like fenugreek and mustard
  • No artificial colors or preservatives
  • Long shelf life when stored properly
  • Versatile - can be used for rice, snacks and more

How to Use

Mix 2 tablespoons of powder with cooked rice along with a little sesame oil. For best results, temper with mustard seeds, curry leaves, peanuts and asafoetida before mixing with rice.

Vangi Bath Powder

₹179

Our aromatic Vangi Bath powder is a special blend of spices including coriander, cumin, chana dal, urad dal, dried red chilies, and other secret ingredients that bring out the authentic flavors of this traditional brinjal rice dish.

Benefits

  • Rich in protein from lentils and dal
  • Contains digestive spices like cumin and coriander
  • No MSG or artificial flavor enhancers
  • Perfectly balanced spice level
  • Can be used with other vegetables besides brinjal

How to Use

Sauté brinjals in oil until tender, add cooked rice and 2 tablespoons of Vangi Bath powder. Mix well and serve hot. Garnish with fresh coriander and fried cashews for extra flavor.

Mango Pickle

₹249

Our traditional mango pickle is made with fresh raw mangoes, mustard seeds, fenugreek, red chili powder, turmeric and other spices, all preserved in sesame oil. This pickle matures over time, developing deeper flavors.

Benefits

  • Rich in vitamin C from raw mangoes
  • Contains probiotics that aid digestion
  • No artificial preservatives - naturally preserved
  • Source of healthy fats from sesame oil
  • Long shelf life when stored properly

How to Use

Enjoy a small portion with rice, roti, or as a condiment with any meal. Start with a small amount as it's quite flavorful. Store in a cool, dry place and use a clean, dry spoon to maintain freshness.
